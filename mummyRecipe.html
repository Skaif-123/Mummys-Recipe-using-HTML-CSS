Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  1. Nestlé MILKMAID Mini
  2. 2 cups (200 gm)
  3. Flour
  4. 600 gms
  5. Grated Paneer
  6. 200 gm
  7. Sooji/Rava
  8. 1½ tsp
  9. Baking Powder
  10. 1½ tsp
  11. Baking Soda
  12. For Frying
  13. Oil
  14. 2 litres
  15. Water
  16. 1 kg
  17. Sugar
  18. 6-8 nos
  19. Coarsely Crushed Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Combine sugar and water in a pan and bring to a boil to create the syrup. Remove from heat, add cardamom, and set aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix gently to form a soft dough, avoiding over-kneading.
  3. Divide the dough into 30-35 portions and gently shape them into round balls.
  4. Heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until they turn golden brown.
  5. Transfer the fried Gulab Jamuns into the cooled sugar syrup. Once all the Gulab Jamuns are added, bring the syrup to a boil again briefly, then remove from heat.
  6. Enjoy your homemade Gulab Jamun warm, garnished with your favourite toppings.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  1. Milk
  2. 1 litre
  3. Lemon Juice
  4. 2 tbsp
  5. Water
  6. 4 cups (1 litre)
  7. Sugar
  8. 2 cups (400 gm)
  9. Cardamom Powder
  10. ½ tsp
  11. Rose Water
  12. 1 tsp (optional)

Recipe

  1. Boil the milk in a pan. Once it starts boiling,
  2. lower the flame and add lemon juice gradually while stirring. This will curdle the milk.
  3. Strain the curdled milk using a muslin cloth to separate the whey from the chhena (curdled solids). Wash the chhena with cold water to remove any lemon flavor.
  4. Knead the chhena for 8-10 minutes until it forms a smooth and soft dough.
  5. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls, ensuring there are no cracks.
  6. In a deep pan, combine sugar and water, and bring it to a boil to make the sugar syrup. Add cardamom powder for flavor.
  7. Gently add the chhena balls into the boiling sugar syrup. Cover the pan and cook on medium heat for 15 minutes, ensuring the syrup doesn't boil over.
  8. Once the Rasgullas double in size and become spongy, remove from heat. Let them cool completely in the syrup.
  9. Serve chilled with a drizzle of rose water for added flavor.
